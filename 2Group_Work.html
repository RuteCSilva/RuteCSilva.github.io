Assignment 2 * Unit 11 * Focus on CNN

Teamwork is an essential aspect of most projects, and in my opinion, it is one of the most efficient ways to achieve the desired outcome. In this project, I played a crucial role by contributing to the theoretical foundation with reading materials and then creating the presentation structure and design. These may seem like small contributions, but they are important in providing a solid foundation for the rest of the team to build upon.

Alberto, having a strong background in coding, completed the Neural Network Models for Object Recognition, which is a complex and challenging task. However, I was still involved in the process by meeting with the team on Zoom to discuss how to improve the model and debug the code aiming to implement what was being taught through the seminars. This was a great source of professional growth.

Overall, I believe that this project highlights the importance of teamwork and collaboration. Each member of the team has a unique set of skills and knowledge, and by working together, you can achieve something that would have been impossible to accomplish individually. It is also essential to recognize that every contribution, no matter how small, plays a crucial role in the success of the project.
