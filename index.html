I am passionate about learning, connecting fields of knowledge to develop my professional practice.
I went to college to study physics and became a weather forecaster with the Portuguese Air Force.
I had my first child and discovered a whole new world; a world of play and fun. I realized I wanted to know more about child development and learning so I went back to university to study education.
Neuroscience, particularly non-pharmacological cognitive enhancement, led me to Goldsmith University where I completed my MSc in Psychology, Genetics and Education.
More recently, I decided to learn more about Artificial Inteligence and I have started a postgrad with UoE.
